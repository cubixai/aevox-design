A plain-language guide to the AeVox look — for decks, social posts, print, Figma,
or Canva. No code needed: copy the hex codes, grab the three fonts, follow the
spacing. **One rule — cyan is the only accent.**

## Color

Cyan is the single accent: buttons, links, highlights. Inks are text. Surfaces are
backgrounds. The status colors are for states only (success, pending, error,
info) — never decoration. Copy the hex straight into Figma, Canva, or your slides.

| Color | Light | Dark | Use for |
|---|---|---|---|
| Cyan / accent | `#0A9CB8` | `#22D3EE` | Primary buttons, links, highlights, focus |
| Ink — text | `#1F2A38` | `#ECEEF2` | Headlines + body copy |
| Muted ink | `#8A97A7` | `#646A73` | Captions, labels, metadata |
| Canvas | `#EEF1F6` | `#0C0E12` | Page / slide background |
| Card | `#FFFFFF` | `#1C1F25` | Panels, cards, sheets |
| Live — green | `#0FAE9A` | `#34E0C4` | Success, active, on-track |
| Train — amber | `#E0930F` | `#F9C652` | Pending, in-progress |
| Warn — red | `#E54848` | `#F87171` | Error, failed, critical |
| Idle — indigo | `#6366F1` | `#7C89F0` | Info, secondary, draft |

## Type

Three free Google fonts. Headlines in **Bricolage Grotesque**, body in **Hanken
Grotesk**, numbers / labels / code in **IBM Plex Mono**.

| Role | Font | Use for |
|---|---|---|
| Display | Bricolage Grotesque | Headlines, titles |
| Body | Hanken Grotesk | Paragraphs, UI text, labels |
| Mono | IBM Plex Mono | Numbers, data, code, eyebrow labels |

**Sizes** (slides / Figma): headlines 32–56px bold · subheads 20–24px · body
15–16px · captions 12–13px. Headlines sit tight (letter-spacing ≈ −2%); mono
labels are UPPERCASE with ≈ +8% spacing.

· [Hanken Grotesk](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Hanken+Grotesk)
· [IBM Plex Mono](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/IBM+Plex+Mono).

## Logo

Use the AeVox mark on a light or dark surface. Give it clear space (≈ its own
height all around), keep it its own color, and place it on a clean background.
Never rotate, stretch, recolor, add shadows/effects, or box it in.

## Shape & spacing

Soft, generous corners — cards ≈ 18px, buttons ≈ 13px, pills fully round. Keep
breathing room: pad cards 20–24px and stack related items 8–12px apart. The look
should feel calm, never cramped.

## Do & don't

**Do** — keep cyan as the single accent · use the three brand fonts everywhere ·
dark text on light surfaces (and light on dark) · give elements room with soft
corners and generous padding.

**Don't** — recolor, rotate, or stretch the logo · add a second accent or
off-brand colors · swap in arbitrary fonts · crowd elements into tight,
hard-edged boxes.
